About Us


We are an independent, community run website with the purpose of improving the Speech and Debate community. We wanted to provide a safe space for Speech and Debaters to share their opinions and experiences at national tournaments. Our goals are two-fold:

  • Collect and provide information about national tournaments to everyone
  • Promote and facilitate change among tournaments with the goal of improving tournament quality.


    • How We Rate Tournaments

    Judging Pool indicates the quality of the judges at tournaments. Please note that judges that vote against you are not necessarily bad judges.


    Accomodations includes the quality of tournament hotels, venues, and amenities.


    Location specifies the quality of the area surrounding the tournament. For example, safety and convenience are things to consider when evaluating location.


    Scheduling represents how well tournaments were run and scheduled. This may include the timing of rounds and wait perionds between rounds.


    Overall is a holistic rating that reviewers feel best represents the overall quality of a tournament. This may reflect Judging Pool, Accomodations, Location, or Scheduling
